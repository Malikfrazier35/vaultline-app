Pricing questions

Is there really a 14-day free trial?

Yes. Full access to all features on your selected plan for 14 days. No credit card required to start. Cancel anytime during the trial with no charge.

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.

Do you offer annual billing?

Yes. Annual plans save 10% across all tiers. Enterprise customers can also negotiate multi-year contracts with additional discounts.

What happens if I exceed my plan limits?

We notify you well before you hit a limit and give you the option to upgrade. We never cut off access or charge overage fees without your approval.

Is my data secure?

Yes. SOC 2 Type II controls, bank-grade encryption at rest and in transit, and granular access controls. Your financial data never leaves our secure infrastructure.

Do you offer nonprofit or startup discounts?

Yes. We offer 30% discounts for registered nonprofits and early-stage startups (pre-Series A). Contact us to apply.
